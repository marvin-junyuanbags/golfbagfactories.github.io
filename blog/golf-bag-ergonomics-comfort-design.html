‚öñÔ∏è Weight Distribution Science

Optimal Load Distribution

Shoulders (40-50%)

Primary load-bearing area with broad muscle support and natural strength.

Back/Torso (30-40%)

Secondary support through back panel contact and core engagement.

Hips/Waist (10-20%)

Supplementary support for heavy loads and extended carrying.

High Center of Gravity

Increases instability and requires more energy to maintain balance, leading to faster fatigue.

Low Center of Gravity

Improves stability and reduces energy expenditure, but may affect club accessibility.
