Maintenance & Care Tips

Regular Maintenance

  • Weekly: Empty all pockets and wipe down exterior
  • Monthly: Deep clean with mild soap and water
  • Seasonally: Inspect zippers and treat with lubricant
  • Annually: Professional inspection and repair if needed

Storage Best Practices

  • Store in cool, dry place away from direct sunlight
  • Keep zippers partially open for air circulation
  • Use bag stand or hang to maintain shape
  • Remove all clubs and accessories during storage
