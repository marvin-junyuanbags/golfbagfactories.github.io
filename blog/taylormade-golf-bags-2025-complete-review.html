FlexTech Stand Technology

FlexTech technology provides superior stability and smooth operation

How FlexTech Works

  • Flexible hinge system adapts to uneven terrain
  • Automatic leg deployment when bag is lifted
  • Enhanced stability on slopes and rough ground
  • Smooth retraction for easy carrying

Benefits Over Traditional Stands

  • 40% more stable than conventional systems
  • Reduced wear and tear on mechanism
  • Quieter operation during play
  • Better weight distribution

Pro Tip

The FlexTech system works best when you lift the bag by the top handle. This automatically engages the stand mechanism for optimal performance.
