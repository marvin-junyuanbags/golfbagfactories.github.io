Embroidery Techniques

🧵 Traditional Embroidery

Classic thread embroidery for names, initials, and simple designs with exceptional durability and professional appearance.

  • • Thread count: 40-120 weight options
  • • Color options: 500+ thread colors
  • • Stitch density: 3,000-15,000 stitches
  • • Durability: 10+ years with proper care

✨ 3D Puff Embroidery

Raised embroidery technique creating dimensional text and logos with foam backing for premium appearance.

  • • Foam thickness: 2-6mm options
  • • Enhanced visibility and texture
  • • Premium brand presentation
  • • Ideal for logos and monograms

🌟 Metallic Thread Embroidery

Luxurious metallic threads in gold, silver, and copper for premium customization with reflective properties.

  • • Metallic options: Gold, silver, copper
  • • Light-reflective properties
  • • Premium luxury appearance
  • • Special care requirements

🎭 Appliqué Embroidery

Fabric pieces sewn onto the bag surface for complex designs, logos, and multi-color applications.

  • • Fabric material options
  • • Complex multi-color designs
  • • Cost-effective for large logos
  • • Layered design possibilities
