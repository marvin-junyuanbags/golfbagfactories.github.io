Size & Weight Guide

📏 Sizing Specifications

Women's golf bags are available in various sizes to accommodate different playing styles, physical requirements, and transportation preferences.

🎒 Carry Bags

Dimensions:

  • Height: 32-34 inches
  • Diameter: 8-9 inches
  • Weight: 2.5-3.5 lbs
  • Dividers: 4-6 way

Best For: Walking rounds, lightweight preference

🛒 Cart Bags

Dimensions:

  • Height: 34-36 inches
  • Diameter: 9-10 inches
  • Weight: 3.5-4.5 lbs
  • Dividers: 6-9 way

Best For: Cart use, maximum storage

🎯 Stand Bags

Dimensions:

  • Height: 33-35 inches
  • Diameter: 8.5-9.5 inches
  • Weight: 3-4 lbs
  • Dividers: 5-7 way

Best For: Versatile use, stability

✈️ Travel Bags

Dimensions:

  • Length: 48-50 inches
  • Width: 12-14 inches
  • Weight: 4-6 lbs
  • Wheels: 2-4 wheel

Best For: Air travel, protection

Buying Considerations

🛍️ Smart Shopping Strategy

Selecting the perfect women's golf bag requires balancing personal preferences, functional needs, budget constraints, and long-term durability expectations.

💡 Key Decision Factors

  • Playing Frequency: Consider how often you play and in what conditions
  • Transportation Method: Walking, cart use, or travel requirements
  • Storage Needs: Equipment volume and organization preferences
  • Style Preferences: Color, design, and aesthetic priorities
  • Budget Range: Initial investment and long-term value
  • Physical Considerations: Strength, height, and comfort factors
