Less is More: The Best Sunday Golf Bags for the Minimalist Golfer in 2025

Published on March 22, 2025 | By The Minimalist Golfer

In an era of oversized tour bags and feature-heavy cart bags, the humble Sunday bag is making a major comeback. These ultra-lightweight, minimalist bags—often called "pencil bags"—are designed for golfers who want to embrace the simplicity of the game. Perfect for a quick nine, a trip to the driving range, or a par-3 course, a good Sunday bag is a joy to carry.

Why You Need a Sunday Bag

Why add a Sunday bag to your collection? The reasons are compelling:

  • Ultra-Lightweight: Most Sunday bags weigh under 3 pounds, making them incredibly easy to carry for 9 or 18 holes.
  • Encourages Minimalism: They force you to think about what you truly need, often leading to more creative shot-making with a half-set of clubs.
  • Perfect for Practice: Ideal for taking a few clubs to the range or practice green without lugging your main bag.
  • Great for Travel & Par-3 Courses: Their small profile makes them easy to pack for a trip or for a quick round on a short course.
  • The "Cool" Factor: There's an undeniable old-school cool to carrying a slim pencil bag over your shoulder.

Our Top Sunday Bag Picks for 2025

1. Loma Bag by Sunday Golf

The Loma bag started the modern Sunday bag revolution. Weighing just 1.95 lbs, it can hold 6-7 clubs, has a built-in stand, and features a valuables pocket and an insulated drink pouch. It's the perfect blend of modern features and minimalist design.

See the Loma Bag in Action

2. Titleist Premium Carry Bag

For the golfer who appreciates luxury, the Titleist Premium Carry Bag delivers. Made from high-quality materials, it has a classic, unstructured "pencil bag" look but with modern touches like mini legs and premium zippers. It’s as much a style statement as it is a golf bag.

3. Ping Moonlite Carry Bag

The Ping Moonlite has been a favorite for years. It's incredibly light (under 2.5 lbs) and features a clever stand-up strap that keeps the bag off wet grass when you set it down. With a 4-way top, it offers a bit more organization than a traditional single-divider bag.

What to Pack in Your Sunday Bag

The key is to carry only the essentials. A typical half-set might include:

  • Driver or 3-Wood
  • A few key irons: e.g., 5-iron, 7-iron, 9-iron
  • A wedge: Pitching or Sand Wedge
  • Putter
  • A handful of balls, tees, a glove, and a ball marker.

This setup is more than enough to enjoy a round and will challenge you to hit different types of shots.
