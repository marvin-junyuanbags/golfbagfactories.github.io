Maintenance & Care for Customized Bags

Proper maintenance preserves your customization investment

Customized golf bags require special care to preserve their appearance and functionality. Different customization elements need specific maintenance approaches to ensure longevity.

Embroidery Care

Cleaning

Use mild soap and soft brush, avoid harsh chemicals

Drying

Air dry away from direct sunlight to prevent fading

Protection

Avoid snagging on rough surfaces or sharp objects

Material-Specific Care

Leather

Condition regularly, protect from moisture

Synthetic Materials

Wipe clean with damp cloth, check for wear

Tech Components

Keep dry, check connections, replace batteries

Maintenance Schedule

After Each Round:
  • • Wipe down exterior
  • • Empty all pockets
  • • Check for damage
  • • Air dry if wet
Monthly:
  • • Deep clean all surfaces
  • • Condition leather parts
  • • Check hardware tightness
  • • Inspect customizations
Seasonally:
  • • Professional inspection
  • • Replace worn components
  • • Update tech features
  • • Storage preparation
