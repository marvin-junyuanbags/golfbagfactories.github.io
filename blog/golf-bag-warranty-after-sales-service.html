üîç Common Issue Troubleshooting

Quick Fix Solutions

üîí Stuck Zipper

  1. Apply soap or wax to zipper teeth
  2. Gently work zipper back and forth
  3. Use pliers on zipper pull if needed
  4. Clean debris from zipper track

ü¶µ Stand Won't Deploy

  1. Check for debris in mechanism
  2. Lubricate pivot points
  3. Ensure legs aren't bent
  4. Test spring tension

üéí Strap Issues

  1. Check attachment points for wear
  2. Adjust strap length properly
  3. Replace worn padding
  4. Ensure buckles function correctly

üíß Water Damage

  1. Remove all contents immediately
  2. Dry thoroughly in ventilated area
  3. Use fans to accelerate drying
  4. Apply mold prevention treatment
